DHAKA CITY

The city I dream of

Dhaka is often ranked among the least liveable cities. I believe, and am certain, we can reshape our city. This city was not built in a day. We talk about its 400 years of history from the Mughal period. But didn't the city exist before that? Did the Mughals come and build the city out of nothing? Actually, Dhaka has come into being over a thousand years. It will take another thousand years to destroy it.
